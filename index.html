Erivando Nogueira

Bio

Here, music serves as a heavy and impactful vessel for timeless narratives. Fusing Dark Country and Folk Rock, the project crafts dense soundscapes that set the stage for rustic, intense vocal performances. While English remains the core language, the artistic exploration delves deeply into the musical textures of Arabic and Slavic (Russian and Ukrainian) cultures, pushing the boundaries of traditional composition. Thematically, the work plunges into the most obscure and unexplored passages of ancient sacred scriptures. The ultimate goal is to revive the visceral power of millennial texts and epic tales through a dark, immersive, and authentic atmosphere.
